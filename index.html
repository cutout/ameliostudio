Beatport Live

Live is the lastest product on the Beatport roadmap, a streaming platform for DJs to broadcast their live sets.

In 2004, Beatport began as an online store and news hub for electronic music culture. When it was purchased by SFX in 2013, it became part of a larger umbrella that included ticketing, live events and streaming music. As more energy was being placed around large festivals, SFX strategized how to develop tools to deliver the live experience to fans around the world.

Case Study

Readability

A popular bookmarklet that grew into a reading platform and eventually a #1 spot in the App Store.

The problem Readability aimed to solve was simple enough: to clean up noisy, distracting web content. The original bookmarklet was an Arc90 lab experiment that removed ads and marginal content with a click, rendering the article as a clean, simple, single column of text with modern typography.

In 2011, Arc90 employees formed a small team to develop Readability as a full-scale reading platform. After launch, its disruptive profit-sharing model with publishers made the New York Times and CNN and started a larger conversation about supporting journalism. Hoefler Frere-Jones agreed for the first time to have their type rendered as a webfont, greatly enhacing the legibilty in the iOS app.
